Who am I ?

My name is Yann, I am a 23 years old french computer engineering student at ISIMA.
Thanks to multiple work-study programs, I already have over 3 years of professionnal experience, and I am currently working at the company CGI.

Employment history

September 2024 – Present

Web Development Apprentice, Team « TMA Open » at CGI. Clermont-Ferrand, France.

Currently involved in developing web applications for companies and associations, such as the ALUMNI association of Clermont School of Business, or Michelin. We work in teams of around 4 developers, using most of the time Angular for the front-end and Java Spring Boot for the back-end. We devide the work using Agile methodologies, and use GitLab for version control and CI/CD. Each developer is responsible for the complete development cycle of their features. This way, I am able to work on both front-end and back-end tasks. You can see some of my work here.

September 2023 – September 2024

DevOps Apprentice, Team « TMA Open » at CGI. Clermont-Ferrand, France.

Successfully deployed a web application designed to help Michelin workers. This application is an AI chatbot that assists employees in redacting and finding documents. It's currently the biggest project that I worked on at CGI, as I was involved in it for 6 months. As a DevOps technician, I was responsible for setting up and maintaining the CI/CD pipelines using GitLab, as well as managing the deployment and monitoring of the application on the production servers using Docker and Kubernetes. I also had the opportunity to work on the back-end of the application, using the .Net Framework (C#). Thus, I had the opportunity to play around with the first version release of the AI development framework Semantic Kernel developed by Microsoft. I learned a lot about the ins and outs of IA development. You can learn more about this project here.
I spent the rest of this apprenticeship working on various smaller projects, mainly focusing on DevOps tasks such as setting up CI/CD pipelines, and securing deployments.

August 2022 - May 2023

IOT Technician, Embedded Systems Unit at ITMI. Sept-Iles, Canada.

This position is my very first paid job in the tech industry. I was hired after my internship at ITMI, and I worked there part-time while pursuing my studies. My main role was designing and 3D-printing casings for various embedded systems. I was working closely with development teams to understand their needs, and collect requirements for the casings. I used CAD software, and had multiple 3D printers at my disposal to create prototypes and final products.

January 2022 - May 2022

Intern IOT Technician, Embedded Systems Unit at ITMI. Sept-Iles, Canada.

During this 5 month internship, I successfully developed a connected hydroponic bench. It's a system designed to grow plants efficiently in a continuous water flow. I equiped this bench with IoT sensors, and implemented real-time monitoring, using microcontrollers and a data visualization system. See more about this project here.
